About me

I am a passionate designer, programmer, problem-solver, and someone who cares deeply about fostering empathy in the workplace and creating meaningful, lasting impacts on people's experiences through thoughtful design. Originally from South Korea, I joined the Robbins Family in Nebraska in 2014. This diverse cultural background has given me a unique perspective that I bring to my design work, enhancing my ability to create empathetic and impactful solutions.

My Journey

My journey began with earning an Associate Degree for Transfer in Computer Science at a community college, which laid the foundation for my technical skills. At UCSD, I became fascinated by UX/UI design and its potential to enhance user experiences, combining this interest with my coding background to explore innovative and impactful solutions.

My Approach

As a user experience designer, my approach focuses on creating innovative, data-driven solutions that deliver real impact. Leveraging my background in Computer Science, I combine technical expertise with design principles to develop seamless user experiences. By utilizing diverse UX tools and methodologies, I guide smart business decisions through goal alignment and problem-framing. This ensures that each design is functional, intuitive, and value-driven, enhancing the digital experience for all users and fostering meaningful interactions.
